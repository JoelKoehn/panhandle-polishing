Why Aluminum Polishing

Aluminum isn't chrome, and it doesn't polish like chrome. It's a softer metal that oxidizes differently, so it takes different compounds, pads, and technique to cut through oxidation and bring up a true mirror finish without damaging the metal underneath. We focus specifically on aluminum so your wheels, tanks, and trim get treated the right way.
